Empower Young Innovators in Visakhapatnam

Hack Club is organizing 200 hackathons globally. We are hosting the Visakhapatnam edition, and we are seeking support to provide laptops to students who currently do not have access to one.

About Hack Club

Hack Club is a global nonprofit organization supporting student-led coding communities. Students build meaningful projects and gain hands-on technical experience through hackathons conducted worldwide.

The Situation in Visakhapatnam

We are organizing a hackathon in a school where many students do not have personal laptops. These students are enthusiastic and eager to learn, but lack access to devices required to build projects.

What We Need

Old Laptops Which You Don't Use

If you have an unused or old laptop at home or in your organization, donating it can directly empower a student to participate in this hackathon.

Device Support

Functional laptops (even older models) that can run basic programming tools are sufficient for students to build meaningful projects.

Financial Contribution (Optional)

Donations can help refurbish donated laptops or arrange temporary devices for the event.

Sponsor Benefits

Impact Satisfaction

You will feel that your unused laptop is being used by students to create projects, giving you a strong sense of contribution and gratitude.

Activity Tracking

You will receive updates and track of the activities students build and develop using your sponsored laptop.
